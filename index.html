About Nora

Welcome to Nora’s Arch, where brows aren’t just shaped, they’re sculpted to perfection. As the owner and operator, I bring over 17+ years of hands-on experience in eyebrow artistry, earning a reputation as a true perfectionist and master of my craft. My goal is simple: to enhance your natural beauty through precise, custom-tailored eyebrow shaping that flatters your individual features.

I’ve built a loyal clientele by consistently delivering exceptional results in a clean, welcoming, and professional environment. Every appointment is a personalized experience, no rushed sessions or one-size-fits-all treatments. Whether you’re looking for subtle refinement or a complete brow transformation, you can expect expert technique, hygienic practices, and a genuine passion for what I do. At Nora’s Arch, your brows are in the hands of someone who treats them like art.
